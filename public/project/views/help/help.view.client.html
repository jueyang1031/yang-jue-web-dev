What is FoodU?

FoodU is an one-stop online web application food explorer and trackers. Shoppers could search for store food products to learn ingredients and nutrients knowledge.

Athletes, dietitians or users who are in fitness or on a diet could record and plan their meal to track daily nutrients intake. Users share and explore healthy meal plan on FoodU in order to pursue a healthier life.


Search Product

Clicking on "Search for nutrients of food" on homepage, you could enter any text in search box to search for food product. If there are matched products, they will be showed below. Browse the list and click on " view product" to view details including nutrients fact.


Sign in, Sign up and Sign out

From the top navigation bar, click on Sign in to login to FoodU or click on Sign up to register a new FoodU account. After login, you could access to meal planner. To sign out, click on Sign out next to username in the navigation bar.


Meal Plan List

After login to FoodU, you will be able to view and edit your meal plans. Meal plans are listed in descending order of plan date. Click on "Meal planner and food tracker" on homepage or click through "My meal plan" under your username to view all your meal plans. To view the meal plan detail, click on the title.


Create New Meal Plan

Click on the in the side bar of meal plan list to create a new meal plan. Plan Date and Meal Plan Title are required. Click on Add meal to add meal to the plan. Click on next each meal to remove it from plan. You must add at least on meal for the meal plan. Each meal must have a time slot and meal title. Search and select food to add it to the meal. There must be at least one food for the meal. Click on on the side bar to save the meal plan. Click on or to back to meal plan list. Click on to clear the form.


Edit Meal Plan

From the meal plan list, click on next to meal plan title to edit it. Click on on the side bar to save the meal plan. Click on or to back to meal plan list.


Delete Meal Plan

Check one or more meal plans on meal plan list and click on to delete multiple plans. Or in the meal plan edit, click on to delete current plan.


Copy Meal Plan

From the meal plan list, click on next to the meal plan title to copy it.


Search and Copy meal plan

In meal plan list, click on on side bar to search for all meal plans created by other users. view the plan detail and click on " copy to my meal plan". Modify the plan on your requirements and save the plan.


Profile

After login to FoodU, you could view and edit your profile click on My profile under your username. Click the picture to change your avatar.


Follow and Unfollow

Users that you are following are listed in your profile below to basic information. Click on followed to unfollow certain user. Or, in meal plan search, add new user to follow by clicking on next to username.
